About the HELPS program

  1. who can use HELPS?
    » Any student enrolled in any faculty at UTS.

  2. Where is HELPS?
    » HELPS is located on Building 1, Level 5 , room 25

  3. How much does it cost?
    » Services are free of tuition fees for non-credit workshops and individual consultations.

  4. Can you help me with my assignment?
    » Yes. HELPS offers various workshops and individual consultations. For more information, check out our website.

  5. Can you proofread and correct my assignment?
    » No. Our role is not to correct grammar or other errors in an assignment. We can help you develop your own editing strategies.
    You should also use a computer spell-check, find a competent friend and a good dictionary.

  6. Can you help me with the content of my assignment?
    » No. We can’t tell you what to say, we can only help you say it better and more clearly.
    While we’re happy to act as a sounding board for your ideas, content questions require the specialised disciplinary knowledge of lecturers and tutors in your faculty.
    You should take specific content questions directly to them.

  7. My lecturer says I need to improve my grammar. Can you help me?
    » Yes. Please check our website for information on our workshops and Learning resources.

  8. Can you help me with my pronunciation?
    » Yes. Please check our website for information on our English speaking programs, workshops and Learning resources.

  9. Can I practise my seminar presentation with someone?
    » Yes. You can attend our workshops or drop in for an individual consultation session.

About the Special Conditions in Exams

  1. I am not a native English speaker and I feel that I need more time in exams. Can you help?
    » Maybe. You might be eligible to apply for Special Conditions in Exams.

  2. I'm a second/third year student. Can I get Special Conditions in my exams?
    » No. Only first year (1st/2nd semester) students are eligible to apply.

  3. What is the deadline to apply for the Special Conditions?
    » The application closes on the Census date. Click on Special Conditions in Exams for more information.

    4. If you have a question which has not been answered above, please email us: HELPS@uts.edu.au
